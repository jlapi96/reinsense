ReinSense is a science-driven smart wearable platform for horses that enables preventive healthcare through continuous monitoring of stress, pain and early physiological changes — before visible symptoms appear.

How ReinSense works

Sense

A smart wearable integrated into a tail protector continuously monitors vital signs such as heart rate, respiration, basal temperature, movement, location and environmental conditions — comfortably and unobtrusively.

Analyze

ReinSense learns each horse’s individual baseline and identifies stress- and pain-related deviations using trend-based, rule-driven anomaly detection supported by scientific research.

Act

Insights are delivered through the mobile app, enabling owners, trainers and veterinarians to adjust training, care routines or seek veterinary support earlier.

The ReinSense platform

Smart wearable

A modular, tail-mounted device designed for continuous wear, weather resistance and minimal interference with natural horse behaviour.

Mobile app & dashboard

An intuitive app visualizes health trends, activity and recovery, stores medical history and generates automated reports for veterinarians.

Built for preventive care

ReinSense focuses on stress and pain — the root causes of many equine diseases — improving welfare and reducing preventable health issues.
